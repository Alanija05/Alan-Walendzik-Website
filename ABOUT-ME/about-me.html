About Me

Hi, I'm Alan, a passionate and driven individual exploring the dynamic world of cybersecurity. My journey began with an insatiable curiosity for how systems work—and how they can be made more secure. Today, I spend my time sharpening my skills through hands-on challenges and learning resources.

I actively participate in Capture the Flag (CTF) challenges on platforms like picoCTF, which push me to think critically and creatively. Additionally, I follow structured learning paths on TryHackMe, diving deep into topics like penetration testing, network security, and threat analysis.

When I'm not working on solving puzzles or expanding my technical expertise, I enjoy collaborating with like-minded individuals and staying up-to-date on the latest developments in the cybersecurity field. My ultimate goal is to contribute to creating a safer digital world by turning my knowledge into practical, impactful solutions.

Feel free to reach out to connect or explore my work—I’d love to share ideas and grow together.
